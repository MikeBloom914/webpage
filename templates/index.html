About Me

Welcome to my page! My name is Michael Bloom. I have been a Commodity Derivatives trader for over a decade. As a trader at DRW Holdings for 8 years, my passion for trading was ignited. As computers became a more integral part of the business, I expanded my focus and expertise to become a computer programmer after attending a Python / Data Science focused FinTech coding bootcamp. Web Trader

Web Trader is a simulation trading game. You trade paper money. The app keeps track of your balance, p/l and positions of all stocks you buy and sell. Yes, that is an actual picture of me on the left signaling to sell a natural gas derivative on the CME trading floor.

Halite III

Created a bot to compete in the Halite III tournament. Halite is an open source artificial intelligence programming challenge, created by hedge fund Two Sigma. (Shecky is my name on Discord)*Webpage coming soon!

CryptosAbyss

CryptosAbyss is a historical price data analysis project to try and find a fundamental indicator for the price movement of cryptocurrencies. You can sort the table on any data you choose.

Anacott Steel

Anacott Steel is an interactive stock chart graphing visualizer to help compare two or more historical stock prices together. The entire app was all done in Python.
